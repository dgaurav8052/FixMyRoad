Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a pothole? expand_more

You can report a pothole by clicking the "Report a Problem" button on our homepage. You'll be asked to provide the location and a description of the issue. A photo is optional but helpful.

What kind of road issues can I report? expand_more

You can report a variety of issues, including potholes, damaged signs, faded road markings, malfunctioning traffic lights, and debris on the road. If you're unsure, it's better to report it.

How long does it take to fix a reported issue? expand_more

The time to fix an issue varies depending on its severity and the resources available. Urgent safety hazards are prioritized. You can check the status of your report using the ID provided.

How does the whole process work? expand_more

Once you report an issue, it's assigned a unique ID. Our team reviews the report, assesses the severity, and schedules it for repair. You can track the status of your report on our website using the ID. You will be notified once the issue is resolved.

Do I need to create an account to report an issue? expand_more

No, you don't need to create an account to report an issue. Instead, you just have to fill out a form with the necessary details about the problem you've encountered.
