About FixMyRoad

We are dedicated to improving road safety and quality for everyone in our community. Learn more about our mission and our process.

visibility

Our Vision

To create a future where every road is safe, reliable, and well-maintained, contributing to a thriving and connected community.

rocket_launch

Our Mission

To empower citizens to easily report road issues and to ensure these reports are addressed efficiently and transparently by the responsible authorities.

See a problem? Help us fix it.

Your reports are crucial for maintaining safe roads. If you've encountered a pothole, damaged sign, or other road issue, let us know.

How It Works

report

1. Submit a Report

Citizens identify and report road issues through our user-friendly website.

2. We Verify & Prioritize

Our team verifies the report, assesses its urgency, and prioritizes it for action.

3. Work is Dispatched

The issue is assigned to a maintenance crew for repair.

4. Issue Resolved

You get notified once the repair is complete. The road is safe again.
